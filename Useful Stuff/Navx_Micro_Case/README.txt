# Summary

Our FRC team #3244 The Granite City Gearheads got this navX mirco in our First choice order. The files given to us did not print well specially with PLA. The board did not fit and the tabs broke off. We quick sketched this up model similarly from the case supplied by NavX. We plan to integrate mounting holes when we know what we will need. 


MyNavX_Micro_CoverV1.1 - Made the windows to the board larger, Lengthen the standoffs to hold the board down, Opened up the bolt holes for 3mm clearance
